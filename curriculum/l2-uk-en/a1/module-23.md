## Presentation: Reflexive Verbs (-ÑÑ)

Some verbs in Ukrainian end in **-ÑÑ**. This means the action is directed at **yourself** (Reflexive) or simply characterizes the state.
Equivalent to English: "I wash *myself*", "I dress *myself*".

### 1. Conjugation Pattern

When you conjugate, add **-ÑÑ** (after consonants) or **-ÑÑŒ** (after vowels) to the end.
