The Sound of Ukrainian

Ukrainian is often called "The Nightingale Language" (—Å–æ–ª–æ–≤'—ó–Ω–∞ –º–æ–≤–∞) because of its musicality. Unlike English, which often swallows vowels, Ukrainian vowels are crisp, clear, and open.

üé≠ Folklore/Traditions Corner

In Ukrainian folklore, words have power. We don't just speak; we "sing" our thoughts. This dates back to the Kobzars, wandering bards who played the kobza and bandura. They preserved Ukrainian history through oral songs when writing was forbidden.
