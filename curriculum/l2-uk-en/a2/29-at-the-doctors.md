# Warm-up

Feeling unwell? This module teaches essential medical vocabulary — how to describe symptoms, understand the doctor, and get help.

---

# Presentation

## At the Doctor (У лікаря)

### Common Phrases

| Ukrainian | English |
|-----------|---------|
| Що вас турбує? | What's bothering you? |
| У мене болить голова. | I have a headache. |
| Мені потрібен лікар. | I need a doctor. |
| Де болить? | Where does it hurt? |
| Як ви себе почуваєте? | How do you feel? |
