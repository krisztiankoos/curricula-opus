## warm-up

### Why Gender Matters

But knowing the gender isn't enough. You need to know **how the word changes**.
In English, words barely change (cat â†’ cats). In Ukrainian, words change their endings constantly to show their role in the sentence (Cases).

To predict these changes, we group nouns into **4 Families** called **Declensions** (Ð’Ñ–Ð´Ð¼Ñ–Ð½Ð¸).
Think of them as "teams" that follow the same rules.

> ðŸ” **Deep Theory: The System**
>

> But to master Ukrainian (B1+), you need the **Declension System**.
