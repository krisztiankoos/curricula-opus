### Shopping in Ukraine

From modern shopping malls to traditional markets (—Ä–∏–Ω–∫–∏), Ukraine offers diverse shopping experiences. Markets are especially vibrant - the Bessarabsky Market in Kyiv and local bazaars everywhere are full of fresh produce, clothing, and household goods.

- **–ú–æ–∂–Ω–∞ –ø–æ–¥–∏–≤–∏—Ç–∏—Å—è?** - May I look?

> üí° **Did You Know?**
>
> Ukrainian markets (—Ä–∏–Ω–∫–∏) are a cultural institution. Prices are often negotiable, especially for clothing and household items. It's expected to haggle politely! Fresh food prices are usually fixed, but you might get a discount if you buy more.
