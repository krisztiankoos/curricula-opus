# Lesson Content

## warm-up

### The Time Machine

Welcome to Phase A1.3! You can talk about now ("I am reading"). Now let's build a Time Machine. We are going back to **Yesterday**.

In English, past tense is messy: "I walked" (regular), "I went" (irregular), "I bought" (irregular).
In Ukrainian, past tense is **beautifully simple**. There is only ONE pattern.
