# Review: B1 Module 18 - Motion Patterns in Other Verbs

## Executive Summary
This module reveals the "secret code" of Ukrainian vocabulary: how motion prefixes (**ви-**, **пере-**, **при-**) create abstract meanings when combined with common roots like **робити**, **писати**, **кладати**. It effectively teaches students to predict meanings (e.g., **ви-** + **робити** = "work out" -> produce).

I have confirmed full compliance. The "Lego" analogy in the Myth Buster is particularly effective for this concept.
