# A2 - Elementary Level

**Complete - 57 Modules**

A2 level builds on your A1 foundation with deeper grammar and expanded vocabulary. This level covers:

- All 7 Ukrainian cases with extensive practice
- Verbal aspect introduction (perfective vs imperfective basics)
- Comparative and superlative forms
- Complex sentence structures with conjunctions
- Word formation patterns (prefixes, suffixes, roots)
- ~1,050 new vocabulary words

**Prerequisites:** Complete A1 level

**Module count:** 57 modules
