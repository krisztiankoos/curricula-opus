Warm-up: The Frozen Tourist

Imagine you are waiting for a bus in Kyiv during winter. It is -10¬∞C. You are shivering.

Presentation: The Indirect Object

The Dative Case answers the question –ö–æ–º—É? (To whom?). We use it for:

  1. Giving (giving something to someone).
  2. Speaking (speaking to someone).
  3. Feeling (feelings happen to someone).
