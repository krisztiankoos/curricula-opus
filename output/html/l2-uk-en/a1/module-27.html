> üìú **History Bite: Ukrainian Colors**
>
> Traditional Ukrainian clothing favored **red** (—á–µ—Ä–≤–æ–Ω–∏–π) and **white** (–±—ñ–ª–∏–π) - symbols of life and purity. Black appeared in mourning and some regional styles. The blue-yellow flag colors became prominent after independence. Today, blue-yellow accessories are patriotic fashion!
