# Describing Things

> **Note:** Nouns are the "bosses". Adjectives are the "assistants". The assistant must always wear the same uniform (gender) as the boss!

## Warm-up: The Colorful World
Look around you. Nothing is just "an object". Everything has a quality.
- The coffee is **hot** (or cold).
- The book is **interesting** (or boring).
- The car is **fast** (or slow).

In English, the word "good" never changes. *Good boy. Good girl. Good mood.*
In Ukrainian, the word changes to match the gender of the thing it describes. This rhyme (agreement) makes the language sound musical.

## Presentation: Adjective Agreement

- *Ğ’ĞµĞ»Ğ¸ĞºĞ° Ğ¼Ğ°ÑˆĞ¸Ğ½Ğ°* (Big car).

For now, stick to **Adj + Noun** order. It is neutral and safe.
