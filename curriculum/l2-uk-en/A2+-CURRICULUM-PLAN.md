## Purpose of A2+ Level

A2+ is a **bridge level** between A2 and B1 that addresses the vocabulary gap. After completing A1 (456 words) and A2 (366 words), learners have 822 words - far below the ~1,800 needed for B1.

**A2+ focuses on:**
1. **Word Formation** - Prefixes, suffixes, and root families to multiply vocabulary
2. **Vocabulary Expansion** - Deeper coverage of practical domains
3. **Collocations** - Common word combinations for natural speech

---

## Prerequisites from A2

Before starting A2+, learners have completed:
- All 7 grammatical cases
- Verbal aspect basics (perfective/imperfective)
- Motion verbs (basic)
- Conditionals (real and unreal)
- Comparative and superlative adjectives
- Relative clauses, complex conjunctions
- 822 vocabulary words (cumulative)
