# Curricula Opus

**A Complete A1-C2 Ukrainian Language Learning Platform**

Based on the **Ukrainian State Standard 2024** and aligned with **CEFR guidelines**.

---

## What is Curricula Opus?

Curricula Opus provides a comprehensive **480-module pathway** for learning Ukrainian as a second language. From your first Cyrillic letter to native-level literary analysis, every lesson is designed with pedagogical rigor and cultural authenticity.

### Our Approach

- **Theory-First Learning** — Understand the "why" behind grammar patterns, not just memorization
- **Cultural Immersion** — Authentic materials, folklore, literature, music, and historical context
- **Interactive Practice** — Every lesson includes 8-16 activities: quizzes, fill-ins, matching, translation, and more
- **Progressive Immersion** — Gradually increase Ukrainian exposure from 25% (A1) to 98% (C2)

## Curriculum Overview

| Level | Description | Modules | Status |

| **A1** | Beginner — Cyrillic alphabet, basic grammar, 5 cases | 30 | In Development |
| **A2** | Elementary — All 7 cases, aspect intro, comparison | 50 | In Development |
| **B1** | Intermediate — Aspect mastery, motion verbs, complex sentences | 80 | In Development |
| **B2** | Upper-Intermediate — Passive voice, participles, style | 125 | Planned |
| **C1** | Advanced — Full complexity, registers, dialects | 115 | Planned |
| **C2** | Mastery — Native-level proficiency, literary creation | 80 | Planned |

**Total:** 480 modules | ~11,000 vocabulary words
